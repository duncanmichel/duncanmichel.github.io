I spent eight years as an Army officer, including time running cyber operations at USCYBERCOM, before moving into the private sector. The military background shows up in how I approach problems more than in anything I write about directly.

This blog covers detection engineering, offensive security research, and the OSCP journey — written for practitioners, by a practitioner.
